Recipe

Directions

  • 1/3 cup light corn syrup
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/3 white sugar
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 (3.5 ounce) pckages microwave popcorn, popped

Steps

  1. Bring corn syrup, honey, and sugar to a boil in a saucepand; cook at a boil for 2 minutes. Immediately remove from heat and stir peanut butter and vanilla into syrup mixture until peanut butter has melted completely.
  2. Pour popcorn into a large bowl; pour sauce over popcorn and stir until evenly coated. Allow to cool completely and break into chunks to serve.
